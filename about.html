What Is WaidSoko?

WaidSoko is a classified global marketplace platform designed to connect businesses, products, services, suppliers, and customers through intelligent digital commerce systems.

The platform focuses on simplifying business operations, improving marketplace accessibility, and creating smart business tools for modern entrepreneurs and organizations.

Why StokPailot Was Created

StokPailot was created by WaidSoko to solve inventory management challenges faced by businesses across different industries.

The platform helps businesses monitor inventory, manage orders, analyze growth, reduce stock problems, and maintain efficient operational workflows using smart inventory technology.
